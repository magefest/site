MageFest 2022

An annual minecraft modjam for magic mods.

This year's theme is... *drumroll* Wizardry!



Rules

1. Mods must be created and submitted during the time of the modjam and source code must be available on GitHub or GitLab.

2. No work must be done on the mod prior to the starting date except for ideas and concept art.

3. At the end of the modjam, winners will be decided at the deadline and are final

4. You are free to update your mod after the deadline, but do note rule 3.

5. Teams are allowed, but with a limit of 4 members

6. Submissions should fit the theme of MageFest

7. Submissions must be completed before the deadline with the exception that, on the owner's discretion, an extra day to make the submission survival friendly

8. Submissions should run on a server environment without crashing

9. Third party libraries and dependencies are allowed. You're responsible for any issues they cause.

10. Submissions must be able run on Quilt 1.18.2 (this includes most fabric mods).

11. NO MCreator AT ALL



Times & Dates

MageFest will start on April 30, 2022 12:00 AM BTC and end on May 20, 2022 12:00 AM BTC.
